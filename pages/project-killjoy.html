About the Film

Killjoy is an original short film that explores the intersection of joy and melancholy through visual narrative. The project showcases a complete understanding of cinematic storytelling, from concept development through final post-production.

Creative Vision

The film was conceived as a study in emotional storytelling using visual language rather than exposition. Through careful framing, color palette selection, and pacing, the narrative unfolds in a way that engages the viewer on a visceral level.

Production Details

Cinematography Adobe Premiere Pro Color Grading Sound Design Motion Graphics

Technical Approach

The production demonstrates professional video production standards including:

  • Cinematography: Professional camera work with attention to composition, lighting, and movement
  • Color Grading: Cohesive color palette that supports the emotional narrative
  • Sound Design: Thoughtful audio layering including dialogue, ambient sound, and music
  • Editing: Pacing and rhythm that drives emotional engagement
  • Post-Production: Professional finishing including color correction and effects
