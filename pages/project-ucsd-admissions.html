UCSD Admissions Packet

Comprehensive Admissions Materials for UC San Diego

Challenge

Develop a comprehensive admissions package that communicates UC San Diego's value proposition while maintaining consistency with institutional branding guidelines.

Solution

Created a complete materials system including cover design, content spreads, photography integration, and call-to-action layouts optimized for both print distribution and digital sharing.

Impact

Delivered a scalable design system supporting multiple communication channels and improving prospective student engagement through professional, cohesive branding.

Project Overview

The UCSD Admissions Packet serves as the primary touchpoint for prospective students, communicating the university's academic excellence, diverse community, and exceptional campus resources. This comprehensive project required balancing institutional messaging with student-focused content.

Scope of Work

  • Strategic information architecture and content organization
  • Custom typography design with clear visual hierarchy
  • Photography direction and integration
  • Multi-page layout system (20+ pages)
  • Print specification and production coordination

Design Elements

  • Custom cover design with die-cut elements
  • Program-specific content pages
  • Campus map and facility highlights
  • Student testimonial sections
  • Application timeline and requirements
  • Financial aid information

Results

The admissions packet design increased inquiry response rates and improved brand perception among prospective students. The modular design system allowed for cost-effective updates and variations for different program focuses.
