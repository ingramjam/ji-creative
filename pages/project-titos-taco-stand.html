Small Business Web Presence

Food Service Business

Challenge

Create an online presence for a local taco stand to showcase menu items, location information, and enable customer engagement.

Solution

Developed an attractive web presence featuring menu displays, location information, ordering capabilities, and social media integration.

Impact

Increased customer awareness, simplified menu access, and enabled online ordering functionality for the local business.
