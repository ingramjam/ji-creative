Brown Bag Sandwich Company

Logo & Signage Design for Local Eatery

Challenge

Create a distinctive brand identity for a local sandwich shop in Anchorage, AK that stands out in a competitive food service market while reflecting the business's casual, welcoming atmosphere.

Solution

Designed a custom logo using layered typography combining two distinct fonts with textured elements, complemented by cohesive signage and staff apparel that communicates the brand's personality and values.

Impact

Established a memorable brand presence that became recognizable in the local community, supporting business growth and customer loyalty through consistent visual identity across all touchpoints.

Brand Identity System

Brown Bag Sandwich Company needed a visual identity that communicated quality, freshness, and approachability. The design solution combines custom typography with textural elements to create something uniquely memorable.

Logo Design Process

  • Custom Typography: Merged two complementary fonts with hand-drawn texture overlays
  • Color Palette: Warm, appetizing tones supporting food service positioning
  • Versatility: Designed for both large signage and small packaging applications
  • Legibility: Ensured readability across all scales and reproduction methods

Deliverables

  • Primary logo with variations (full color, monochrome, icon)
  • Logo lockup and spacing guidelines
  • Storefront signage design and specifications
  • Staff uniform shirt designs and graphics
  • Brand guidelines document
  • Supporting collateral templates

Key Features

  • Distinctive custom typeface combination
  • Hand-crafted texture integration
  • Professional signage system
  • Scalable design suitable for multiple applications
