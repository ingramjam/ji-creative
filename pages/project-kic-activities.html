KIC Activities

Interactive Activity Platform for Kick It California

Activity Management System

Challenge

Create an engaging activity platform for Kick It California to help participants discover, register, and participate in health and wellness activities.

Solution

Developed an interactive platform featuring activity discovery, registration, community engagement, and progress tracking for health program participants.

Impact

Increased program participation and engagement through accessible activity discovery and streamlined registration process.

Health Activity Platform

KIC Activities provides an engaging platform for participants to discover and engage with health and wellness activities within the Kick It California program.

Core Features

  • Activity Discovery: Browse and filter health and wellness activities
  • Registration System: Easy sign-up for programs and events
  • Calendar Integration: View schedules and availability
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor personal participation history
  • Community Features: Connect with other participants
  • Resource Access: Information and tips for health activities

Activity Categories

  • Fitness and sports activities
  • Health education workshops
  • Wellness events
  • Group challenges and competitions
  • Community volunteer opportunities

User Benefits

  • Find activities that match interests
  • Simple online registration
  • Track participation and achievements
  • Connect with program community
  • Access to health resources and tips
