UCSD Concept

Admissions Packet Design & Brand Materials

Challenge

Create compelling admissions materials that reflect UC San Diego's academic excellence and vibrant campus culture to attract top-tier prospective students.

Solution

Designed a comprehensive brochure concept featuring clean typography, strategic imagery, and a cohesive visual language that communicates institutional values while appealing to high-achieving students.

Impact

Established brand foundation for UCSD communications with reusable design system and consistent visual identity across materials.

Project Overview

This UCSD admissions packet concept represents a strategic approach to institutional branding. The design system incorporates the university's commitment to academic rigor while maintaining visual accessibility for prospective students and their families.

Design Approach

  • Typography: Clean, modern sans-serif hierarchy for readability and authority
  • Color Palette: University brand colors with strategic accent usage
  • Layout: Grid-based design system ensuring consistency across pages
  • Imagery: Carefully selected campus photography showcasing student life and facilities

Key Components

  • Cover design with institutional messaging
  • Program overview spreads
  • Campus life photography sections
  • Application information and call-to-action
  • Contact and resources directory
