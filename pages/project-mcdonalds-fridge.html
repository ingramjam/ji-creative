Ronald McDonald House - Fill the Fridge

Nonprofit Fundraising Campaign Design

Campaign Design

Challenge

Create compelling marketing materials for a Ronald McDonald House fundraising initiative designed to increase donations and community engagement for families in need.

Solution

Developed a comprehensive campaign design leveraging emotional storytelling, compelling imagery, and clear calls-to-action to encourage community participation and donations.

Impact

Increased fundraising campaign effectiveness and community awareness of Ronald McDonald House's mission and services.

Campaign Design & Execution

The "Fill the Fridge" campaign required thoughtful design that communicates the charitable mission while inspiring community action and support.

Campaign Components

  • Print Materials: Posters, flyers, and signage
  • Digital Assets: Social media graphics and email templates
  • Merchandise: Campaign branded items
  • Donation Materials: Pledge cards and information packets

Design Strategy

  • Emotional storytelling connecting donors with impact
  • Clear message hierarchy and calls-to-action
  • Ronald McDonald House brand integration
  • Multi-channel campaign consistency
  • Cultural sensitivity and inclusivity

Campaign Elements

  • Hero imagery showcasing families and community
  • Impactful messaging around mission and impact
  • Easy donation information and pathways
  • Event promotion and participation details
  • Social sharing and engagement hooks
