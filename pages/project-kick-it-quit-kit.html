Kick It Quit Kit

Downloadable Educational Resource

Quit Kit Resource Document

Challenge

Create an engaging, comprehensive educational resource to support youth in making healthy decisions about substance use, packaged as an accessible downloadable kit.

Solution

Designed a visually engaging PDF resource combining educational content, interactive elements, goal-setting tools, and supportive messaging in an accessible format for young audiences.

Impact

Provided thousands of youth with practical tools and information supporting healthy decision-making and reducing substance use risk factors.

Educational Resource Development

The Quit Kit represents a comprehensive approach to youth health education, combining evidence-based health information with design that resonates with young audiences.

Content Areas

  • Health Information: Substance use facts and health impacts
  • Decision-Making Strategies: Peer pressure resistance and decision tools
  • Goal Setting: Interactive worksheets for personal commitment
  • Support Resources: Crisis hotlines and local services
  • Success Stories: Youth testimonials and experiences

Design Approach

  • Age-appropriate color schemes and typography
  • Engaging infographics explaining health concepts
  • Interactive worksheets and reflection pages
  • Accessible language and visual hierarchy
  • Print and digital optimization

Specifications

  • Format: PDF (printable and digital)
  • Pages: 24-32
  • File Size: Optimized for download
  • Accessibility: Tagged for screen readers
